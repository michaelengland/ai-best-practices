## Content Outline

### Section 1: The Hook — "What if your whole team knew?"
**Key message**: There's a massive gap between using AI and using it well — and even the experts feel it.
**Content**:
- Karpathy hook: Co-founder of OpenAI called AI tools "slop" in October 2025. Two months later: "I've never felt this much behind. This is a skill issue." (Sources: Dwarkesh Podcast, Karpathy on X)
- "What if your whole team knew the trick?"
- 1B+ use AI monthly. <2% use it well. (DataReportal, Reuters)
- 41% of code is AI-generated. 19% longer without understanding. (Second Talent, Addy Osmani)
- "The gap between using AI and using AI well is massive."
**Emphasis**: High
**Speaker notes**: Hook lands fast — 30 seconds max. Stats build urgency. ~90 seconds total.
**Transition**: "So where does your team sit?"

---

### Section 2: The Spectrum — "Most of us are here"
**Key message**: There's a clear progression from beginner to expert, and most people are near the start.
**Content**:
- 7-persona spectrum: Skeptic → Explorer → Whisperer → Strategist → Operator → Orchestrator → Builder
- "Most of us are here." (Explorer)
- "The AI Academy takes you here." (Orchestrator)
**Emphasis**: Medium
**Speaker notes**: Let people find themselves. Don't rush. ~30 seconds.
**Transition**: "Here's how we get there."

---

### Section 3: The Academy — "Two sessions, four levels"
**Key message**: The AI Academy is two practical, hands-on sessions that take you from Explorer to Orchestrator.
**Content**:
- "Two sessions. One hour each. Bring your laptop."
- Workshop 1: Explorer → Strategist — from vague prompts to a persistent AI playbook
- Workshop 2: Strategist → Orchestrator — from doing the work to directing AI teams
- "Not lectures. You'll have ChatGPT open. You'll do the work."
**Emphasis**: High (this is the sell)
**Speaker notes**: Emphasize hands-on. "You'll see the difference in real time." ~90 seconds.
**Transition**: "Ready?"

---

### Section 4: CTA — "Bring your laptop and a task"
**Key message**: Sign up, show up, bring a real task.
**Content**:
- "First workshop: [date]"
- "Bring your laptop and a task you do every week."
**Emphasis**: Medium
**Speaker notes**: Simple, specific, low-commitment. ~30 seconds.
